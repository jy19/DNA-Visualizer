Extra Information

Aligning

The alignment uses the Needleman-Wunsch algorithm, or more commonly known as global alignment. The matches are given a score of 2 while mismatches are given a score of -3. Opening gap penalty is 5 and extending the gap penalty is 2. This is why we see the large regions of black sometimes.

About the DNA

Here are more information about the DNA visualized:

  • Human Chromosome 7: homo sapiens BAC clone RP11-416N13 from chromosome 7
  • Human Chromosome 19: homo sapiens chromosome 19 cosmid R31546
  • Common Chimp: pan troglodytes isolate Yerkes chimp peigree #C0471 genomic scaffold
  • House mouse: mus musculus predicted gene 6185 from chromosome 1
  • Dog: canis lupus familiaris unplaced genomic scaffold whole genome shotgun sequence
